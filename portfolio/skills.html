Skills

I am experienced in providing nurturing and stimulating care for children of all ages, and am able to create a safe and positive environment for them to thrive in. My strong organizational and time-management skills allow me to keep on top of daily routines and activities, ensuring that children are well-fed, clean, and on schedule.

I am also skilled in providing homework help and educational activities that support children's cognitive development. Additionally, I possess strong communication and interpersonal skills, which enable me to effectively communicate with children, parents, and other caregivers. I am proactive, responsible, and attentive to the needs of the children. I am also flexible and able to adapt to different parenting styles and family dynamics.
